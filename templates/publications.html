Abstract

This project focuses on using machine learning, particularly Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) analysis, to detect AI-generated images by identifying synthetic patterns and assessing authenticity. The system aims to distinguish real and synthetic visuals by examining minute pattern inconsistencies and deepfake signatures. Our approach leverages state-of-the-art vision transformers and deep learning architectures to achieve high accuracy in identifying artificially generated content, contributing to the fight against misinformation and digital fraud.
